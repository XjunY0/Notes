## pandas
### 遍历
####  1. `iterrows()`

逐行遍历DataFrame。返回一个 (index, Series) 对。

~~~py
import pandas as pd

df = pd.DataFrame({'A': [1, 2, 3], 'B': [4, 5, 6]})
for index, row in df.iterrows():
    print(index, row['A'], row['B'])
~~~
